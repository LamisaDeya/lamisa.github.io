Lamisa Bintee Mizan Deya

Greetings,

I am Lamisa Bintee Mizan Deya, currently pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh. I am working under the supervision of Associate Professor Abdul Aziz.

My research interests broadly cover multimodal machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. My current focus is on video summarization. This involves processing video input by converting it into audio, followed by transcribing the audio into text. The summarization is then generated in both Bengali and English. Additionally, I analyze the motion within the video and extract key information from frames, incorporating visual content into the final summary. This approach aims to make the models computationally efficient, capable of handling noisy or low-quality data, and resilient in handling scenarios with missing, noisy, or misaligned elements across modalities.

I am deeply honored to have Abdul Aziz as my mentor. His guidance has introduced me to the fascinating world of research and provided me with the comprehensive knowledge that forms the foundation of my current academic journey.
